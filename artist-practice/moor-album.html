Moor: Album Inspired by Wuthering Heights and the Collected Poetry of Emily Brontë

"Moor" is an album of 5 folk songs released in May 2025 under the name Maris Halna, written around the poetry of Emily Brontë and her novel, Wuthering Heights. The album features four original songs and performances, and one cover rendition of Karen Dalton's 1966 "Reason to Believe". The album was released on Spotify, Apple Music, and Youtube.
