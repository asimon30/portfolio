Pokoje Installation, Prague 2022

What essential elements compose starlight on the horizon?

In November 2022, I collaborated with four fellow student artists to create a site-specific installation for the Pokoje Exhibition of Young Art in Prague. Our project explored the spatial and symbolic dimensions of borders—both political and ecological.

We approached the room as a space to challenge the linearity of boundary-making, experimenting with vertical and horizontal planes to evoke dividing-lines as fragile and shifting.

The installation featured a multi-layered video projection: a static horizon line dispersed across suspended translucent sheets, visually “shattering” as it encountered interruptions in the fabric, disrupting the horizon as a universal visual anchor.
